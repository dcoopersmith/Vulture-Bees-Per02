Vulture Bees

The Great Vulture Bee!

Vulture bees, also called carrion bees, live in South America. These bees do not sting. They are in the genus Trigona. They feed on rotting meat.

Vulture bees are colored reddish-brown. They have very powerful jaws, also called mandibles. They use their mandibles to tear off meat. Research has shown that vulture bees prey on up 75 different kinds of animals.

Vulture Bees Eating a Dead Frog

What Vulture Bees Do With Meat They Eat

Vulture bees enter dead prey through the eyes. Then they eat the rotting flesh. Vulture bees vomit the flesh they eat into a storage pot. Later on worker bees will process it more.
