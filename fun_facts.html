Fun Facts

What Do Vulture Bees Eat?

Although vulture bees can eat pollen like regular honeybees, they prefer to eat dead, rotting meat.

Are All Vulture Bees the Same?

No. There are forager vulture bees, and worker vulture bees, and male (drone) vulture bees. But the most important vulture bee is the queen.

Do Vulture Bees Serve Any Purpose in the Environment?

Yes! Vulture bees are very important for eating dead and decaying animals. This protects many other animals and humans from getting diseases from the rotting corpses.
