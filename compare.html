Compare

How Are Vulture Bees the Same As Honey Bees?

  • They both fly.
  • They are both bees.
  • They both live in hives.
  • They both keep larva in their hives.
  • They both make Royal Jelly.
  • They both swarm.
  • They both have queens.

Contrast

How Are Vulture Bees Different Than Honey Bees?

  • Honeybees sting. Vulture bees don't sting.
  • Honeybees make honey. Vulture bees make meat honey.
  • Honeybees are yellow. Vulture bees are reddish-brown.
  • Honeybees vomit nectar. Vulture bees vomit meat.
  • Honeybee length is 0.5 to 0.75 inches. Vulture bees are 0.31 to 0.87 inches.
  • Honeybee honey is tasty. Meat honey is not tasty.
